Action potential amplitude, peak minus resting potential for action potentials elicited by 0.6-ms duration current pulses; sag, % decrease in slope of current–voltage relationship (i.e., input resistance) calculated for final 10 ms of 40-ms hyperpolarizing current pulses with respect to the slope calculated using the peak hyperpolarization; rheobase, current threshold for action potential initiation during 40-ms depolarizing pulse; ramp recruitment rate, average instantaneous firing rate from the first two interspike intervals during the ascending limb of current ramp; ramp derecruitment rate, average instantaneous firing rate from the last two interspike intervals during the descending limb of current ramp; ramp F–I slope, slope of frequency–current relationship on ascending limb of current ramp.
