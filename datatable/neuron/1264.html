Table 1.

Membrane properties of vasopressin neurons in suprachiasmatic nucleus

Parameter Mean
Resting membrane potential, mV −53.5 ± 1.1
Input resistance, GΩ 1.45 ± 0.12
Time constant, ms 29.4 ± 2.3
Action potential amplitude, mV 68.1 ± 2.0

  • Values are means ± SE. For methods of quantification, see text. These data are based on 17 cells recorded at room temperature.
