Table 1. Electrophysiological properties of non-pyramidal cell classes were significantly different from those of pyramidal cells

PC (n = 6) NR-RSNP (n = 36) RA-RSNP (n = 6) R-RSNP (n = 6) FS (n = 5) DS (n = 3) IS (n = 2)
The table shows a summary of the analysed electrophysiological properties of the CA1 pyramidal cells (in stratum pyramidale) and the six subtypes of non-pyramidal cells in s. radiatum (including all cells where electrophysiological characterization was successfully studied at the end of the experiment). Non-pyramidal cells are grouped according to their firing profiles. RMP, resting membrane potential; Rin, input resistance; τ, membrane time constant; AHP, afterhyperpolarization. Each parameter of every interneuron group was compared to the corresponding value of the pyramidal cells. Asterisks indicate parameters that differed significantly (P < 0.05) from those of the pyramidal cells (unpaired t test).
